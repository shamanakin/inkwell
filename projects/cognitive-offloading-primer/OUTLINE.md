# Cognitive Offloading Primer: Outline

---

## Introduction: What Is Cognitive Offloading?

**Purpose**: Hook the reader, define the concept clearly, and establish why this matters.

**Key Points**:
- The phenomenon: we're outsourcing cognitive functions to external systems
- Why this is different from tools (tools extend; offloading replaces)
- The scope: memory, decision-making, regulation, identity, belonging, discernment
- Why this matters: it's changing what it means to be human
- What this primer will explore

**Structure**:
1. Opening: The observation—something is shifting
2. Defining cognitive offloading (with examples)
3. Distinguishing from tools and extensions
4. The scope of what's being offloaded
5. Why this matters now
6. What to expect from this primer

**Estimated Length**: 2,000-3,000 words
